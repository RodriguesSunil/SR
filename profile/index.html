Why Test?

The Myer-Briggs Indicator outlines your personality, strengths and preferences. It identifies career preferences and compatibility with other people. The learning test gives me insight into what learning style I more predominantly use. To be successful in an organisation or a project one has to understand their strengths and weaknesses. Leaders need to understand the strengths and weaknesses of the whole team. Understanding this will form a good team that will work together, to achieve a common goal. Knowing the capabilities and the personalities of the candidates, makes it easier to divide work among the group to achieve optimum results. Knowing the team and the personalities helps you form leadership groups. It helps work out who are the thinkers and the doers. Having a balanced group working together will achieve the best results.
